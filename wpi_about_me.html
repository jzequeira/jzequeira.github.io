About Me

My name is Jose Zequeira, and I am currently attending WPI's Frontiers program for rising juniors and seniors.

I have visited the Worcester area many times before, but I spent most of my life in my home town of Miami, Florida, where I am a rising senior at Belen Jesuit Preparatory School.

I have been involved with my school's robotics team for four years, competing with them in the First Tech Challenge and recently becoming the club president. Our robotics team performed well in the last few years considering how new it is, and because of our team's performance, the school allowed us to start up a new FIRST Robotics Challenge team to allow us to progress to more advanced competitions.
